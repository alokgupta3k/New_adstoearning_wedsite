How to Earn Money from a Website

In today’s digital age, creating a website is one of the best ways to build your online presence and earn money. Websites can generate income through ads, affiliate marketing, product sales, and much more. But the real secret lies in **design, content, and user experience**.

1. Advertising (Google AdSense & Others)

The most popular way to earn money is by displaying advertisements. Google AdSense allows website owners to show relevant ads to their visitors. You get paid every time a visitor views or clicks an ad.

2. Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing allows you to promote products or services on your website. When someone purchases through your referral link, you earn a commission. This works especially well with blogs, product reviews, and niche websites.

3. Selling Digital Products

Digital products like eBooks, courses, templates, and software have become extremely popular. They require effort only once to create and can be sold multiple times, making them a great source of passive income.

If your website gains popularity, brands may approach you to publish sponsored posts. This is a direct way to earn money without relying on ad clicks.
